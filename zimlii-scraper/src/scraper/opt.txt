<section class="akn-section" id="sec_3" data-eid="sec_3">
    <h3>3. Prohibition on destruction, etc., of farm equipment or material</h3>
    <section class="akn-subsection" id="sec_3__subsec_1" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_1">
    <span class="akn-num">(1)</span><span class="akn-content">
    <span class="akn-p" id="sec_3__subsec_1__p_1" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_1__p_1">
    No owner or holder of farm equipment or material shall wilfully demolish, damage, 
    alter or in any other manner impair the farm equipment or material, or cause any 
    other person to demolish, damage, alter or in any other manner impair it, without
    the permission in writing of the 
    <span class="akn-term" data-refersto="#term-acquiring_authority" id="sec_3__subsec_1__p_1__term_1" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_1__p_1__term_1" aria-expanded="false">
    acquiring authority</span>.</span>
    </span>
    </section><section class="akn-subsection" id="sec_3__subsec_2" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_2">
    <span class="akn-num">(2)</span><span class="akn-intro">
    <span class="akn-p" id="sec_3__subsec_2__intro__p_1" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_2__intro__p_1">An owner or holder of farm equipment or material who contravenes subsection (1) shall be guilty of an offence and liable to—</span></span><section class="akn-paragraph" id="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a"><span class="akn-num">(a)</span>
    <span class="akn-intro"><span class="akn-p" id="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a__intro__p_1" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a__intro__p_1">a fine—</span></span><section class="akn-subparagraph" id="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a__subpara_i" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a__subpara_i"><span class="akn-num">(i)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p" id="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a__subpara_i__p_1" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a__subpara_i__p_1">
    equivalent to so much of the amount of the impairment caused to farm equipment or material as is ascertainable in monetary terms; or</span></span></section><section class="akn-subparagraph" id="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a__subpara_ii" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a__subpara_ii"><span class="akn-num">(ii)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p" id="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a__subpara_ii__p_1" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_2__para_a__subpara_ii__p_1">not exceeding level ten; whichever is the greater amount; or</span></span></section></section><section class="akn-paragraph" id="sec_3__subsec_2__para_b" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_2__para_b"><span class="akn-num">(b)</span><span class="akn-content">
    <span class="akn-p" id="sec_3__subsec_2__para_b__p_1" data-eid="sec_3__subsec_2__para_b__p_1">imprisonment for a period not exceeding two years; or to both such fine and such imprisonment.</span></span></section></section></section>
